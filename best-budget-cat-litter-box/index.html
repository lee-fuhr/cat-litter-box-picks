The Nature’s Miracle High-Sided Litter Pan offers ample real estate at a budget price, and the low entry works for cats of all ages. Best for small apartments

When every inch counts, a compact box that tucks into a narrow alcove or beside a toilet makes a big difference. The Petmate Slim Jim Litter Box provides a small footprint without skimping on side height, so litter stays inside and odors are somewhat contained. It’s also lightweight enough to move for quick floor cleaning. Our pick: Petmate Slim Jim Litter Box.

Covered boxes naturally trap smells better than open pans, and a charcoal filter adds an extra layer. The Catit Jumbo Hooded Litter Pan gives you both features at a price that won’t strain your wallet. It’s ideal for owners of one or two cats who want a discreet, tidy-looking box that keeps odors in check. Our pick: Catit Jumbo Hooded Litter Pan.
